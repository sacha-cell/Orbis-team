Who We Are

Boutique Agency. Deep Platform Roots.

Orbis Team is a boutique agency with deep roots in programmatic media and a forward position in the two channels reshaping advertising right now: retail media and connected television.

Our team brings over 50 years of combined programmatic experience across broadcast, digital publishing, e-commerce, consumer retail, and emerging ad technology. We've worked inside the platforms, alongside the networks, and directly with the brands. That means our clients don't pay for a learning curve.

We operate across the full media stack — from audience strategy and platform activation to campaign management and closed-loop measurement. Every engagement is built around outcomes, not activity.

What We Deliver

Six Practice Areas. One Point of Contact.

Retail Media Activation

We activate campaigns across the premier retail media networks — combining commerce-first audience data with programmatic precision to reach in-market shoppers at every stage of the purchase journey. Onsite, offsite, and connected — we manage the full retail media footprint on your behalf.

Connected Television (CTV)

CTV is no longer a brand awareness line item. We execute streaming campaigns that connect to real outcomes — using verified household and behavioral data to reach the right viewers on the right screens, with measurement frameworks that prove the lift.

Programmatic Display & Audience Targeting

We build and execute audience-first programmatic strategies across the open web, mobile, and in-app environments. Our targeting infrastructure draws on first-party, verified third-party, and contextual data — built for the post-cookie world.

Omnichannel Media Planning

The most effective advertising doesn't live in one channel. We coordinate across CTV, display, digital audio, social extensions, and print — building unified plans that deliver consistent messaging and compounding reach across every touchpoint.

Unique Market Solutions

Not every opportunity fits a standard media plan. We bring proprietary and partner-driven solutions to market — including audience environments, inventory access, and creative delivery formats that most agencies simply don't have.

Strategy, Analytics & Measurement

Every campaign begins with a clear brief and ends with accountable proof. We apply multi-touch attribution, incrementality frameworks, and cross-channel reporting to tell you what actually moved the needle — and where to invest next.
